Explore DPL Gallery

DPL Gallery is a collection of different types of art spanning from impressionism to abstract. The art styles we feature the most is abstract, minimalist, and botanical art. However, we lean more into abstract, minimalist, and botanical art. Learn more about our styles!

Abstract art
Art that uses patterns, colors, shapes, and gestural markings rather than trying to accurately illustrate visual reality.
Minimalist art
Art that extends from abstract ideals but in a more simplistic way.
Botanical art
Art that aims to accurately represent whole plants or portions of plants in a way that is both beautiful and realistic.
